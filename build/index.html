Health care, we have a problem.

As science and technology have advanced, modern medicine has taken giant leaps forward. But the health care experience still feels light years behind.

In an era of digital transformation, industries from streaming to shopping have turned simplicity, seamlessness and a personal touch into a modern-day expectation. But when it comes to the most valuable thing people have, their health, life sciences brands aren’t living up to this standard.

It’s the complexity that hurts.

Confusing. Cold. Overwhelming.
Unequal. Outdated. Disconnected.

This is how health care professionals and patients describe the health care system. Something so complex, it can get in the way of people accessing, adhering and benefiting from the life-changing products they so desperately need.

Health care needs more connection

Our mission is to be the difference between what health care is and what it should be. We use machine learning models to fill in insight gaps and human creativity to improve the way people experience life science brands.
